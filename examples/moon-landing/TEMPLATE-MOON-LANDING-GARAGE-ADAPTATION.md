## 🎨 Design Decisions

### Why These Colors?

**Orange Primary:**
- Creates sense of urgency (important for bookings)
- Warm and welcoming (trust building)
- High visibility, action-oriented
- Common in service/automotive industry

**Steel Blue Secondary:**
- Professional, industrial feel
- Represents quality and reliability  
- Contrasts well with orange
- Evokes machinery and precision

**Red Accent:**
- Emergency/urgency messaging
- Attention-grabbing for CTAs
- Automotive industry familiarity

### Why Dark Theme?

- ✅ Modern, professional appearance
- ✅ Makes content pop (especially photos)
- ✅ Reduces eye strain when viewing services
- ✅ Differentiates from traditional "plain white" garage sites
- ✅ High-end, premium feel
